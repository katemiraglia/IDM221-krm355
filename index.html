Contact + About

Hi, I’m Kate, a graphic designer and creative based in Philadelphia, PA. I am currently in my final year of undergrad at Drexel University pursuing a Bachelors of Science in Graphic Design with a minor in User Experience and Interaction Design. I have always had a passion for all things creative from arts and crafts to fine arts and realism to all aspects of design. Design has given me the ability to express my creativity through my work daily, and I have a wide range of design interests, including but not limited to web design, publication design, environmental graphic design, and branding.
